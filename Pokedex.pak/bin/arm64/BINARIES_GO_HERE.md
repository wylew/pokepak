# bin/arm64/

Place the following binaries here (compiled for arm64 / tg5040):

- `minui-list`       — from https://github.com/josegonzalez/minui-list/releases
- `minui-presenter`  — from https://github.com/josegonzalez/minui-presenter/releases

Download the tg5040 release assets and ensure they are executable:

  chmod +x minui-list
  chmod +x minui-presenter
